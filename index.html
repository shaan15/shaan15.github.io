Hi! I'm Shaan Chopra.

I'm a first-year masters student in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Maryland, College Park. I wish to design and develop culturally-responsive technologies for sensitive topics in health, education, and technology and equity.

Once something is a passion, the motivation is there.

Michael Schumacher

My research and academic interests primarily lie in the field of human-computer interaction (HCI) and Information and Communication Technologies for Development (ICTD), with a focus on health, education, and technology and equity. I wish to work in this interdisciplinary domain - combining my knowledge from diverse backgrounds including computer science, machine learning, design, and social science - to design and develop culturally-responsive technologies. I work/ have worked with Dr. Amanda Lazar, Dr. Pushpendra Singh, Dr. Neha Kumar, Dr. Valérie Issarny, Dr. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru (aka PK), Dr. Mayank Vatsa, Dr. Richa Singh, and Dr. Shriram Venkatraman.
I am extremely passionate about sports and fitness. I have played nationals in basketball and discus throw. I have won medals in discus throw (unbeaten streak of 14 gold medals), basketball, shotput, javelin throw, and football at state and intercollege-level events (webstory). I am also an enthusiastic artist and live-sketching is my newly acquired interest. This cover picture was also clicked by me at Pahalgam, Kashmir.
